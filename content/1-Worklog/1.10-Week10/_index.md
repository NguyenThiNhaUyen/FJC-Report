### Week 10 Objectives:
* Understand how AWS services are charged.
* Learn tools for cost monitoring and management.
* Adjust resources to avoid unnecessary costs.

### Week 10 Outcomes:
* Understood how AWS services generate costs over usage time.
* Learned to use Cost Explorer to monitor and estimate expenses.
* Realized that unused resources can still incur charges if left running.
* Began forming a habit of checking costs regularly.
* Recognized that cost management is as important as technical implementation.
